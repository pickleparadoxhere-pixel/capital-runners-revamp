WHO WE ARE

The Speaking Division of CRG

Empowering Communication in Gandhinagar

Capital Runners Gandhinagar (CRG) is more than just a fitness club; it is a holistic community dedicated to overall self-improvement. **Toastmasters CRG** was established as our sister club to help members break speaking anxiety and master public communication.

As a chartered club under Toastmasters International (District 98), we offer a supportive, peer-led sandbox environment where you can practice presentations, tell stories, and learn leading methodologies.

Toastmasters Pathways Program

We follow the official Toastmasters Pathways curriculum. Choose from 11 specialized tracks—including Dynamic Leadership, Presentation Mastery, and Innovative Planning—tailored to support your exact professional ambitions.

Our Speaking Mission

To provide a mutually supportive and positive learning experience in which every member has the opportunity to develop communication and leadership skills, fostering self-confidence and personal growth.

Core TM Values

We stand firmly on the global foundation values of Toastmasters International: Integrity, Respect, Service, and Excellence in everything we practice.

MEETING ANATOMY

Structure of a Meeting

A typical Toastmasters session is a highly structured, 2-hour learning experience divided into three main acts:

Act 1 — Prepared Speeches

Delivering Curated Content

Members prepare and deliver project speeches based on their chosen educational Path. These speeches range from 5 to 7 minutes and focus on specific objectives such as vocal variety, body language, visual aids, or storytelling.

Act 2 — Table Topics

Impromptu Speaking Sandbox

This is the fun and interactive segment where guests and members are invited to speak for 1 to 2 minutes on random, impromptu topics. It trains your brain to think quickly on your feet and respond with structure under pressure.

Act 3 — Evaluations

Constructive Growth Feedback

Every prepared speaker is assigned an evaluator who provides immediate, constructive verbal and written feedback. We also track grammatical accuracy, filler words (Ah-Counter), and strict timeline guidelines (Timer).

HAVE QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any entry fee to attend as a guest?

No. Attending CRG Toastmasters meetings as a guest is completely free of charge. You can attend as a guest as many times as you'd like to experience the format before deciding to join.

Do I need to be a runner with CRG to join the Toastmasters club?

No, the Toastmasters club is open to everyone. While we share a close sisterhood relationship with the running club, anyone in Gandhinagar looking to improve their public speaking is welcome to enroll.

What is the age requirement to join Toastmasters?

To become an official registered member of Toastmasters International and access the Pathways educational portal, you must be 18 years of age or older.
