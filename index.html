Who We Are

We are Gandhinagar’s first organised, timetable-driven running community - built by runners, for runners.

What began with just four passionate enthusiasts has grown organically into a thriving community of active members. United by discipline, consistency, and a love for running, we train together, support each other, and make fitness a shared lifestyle rather than a solitary pursuit.

Join us to embrace your love for running, define your goals, and make a positive impact in your life and the community around you.

Our Mission

To provide a supportive, inclusive, and positive environment for runners of all levels to thrive, discover their potential, and achieve their personal fitness goals.

Our Vision

To create a vibrant, healthy, and closely-knit community of individuals passionate about fitness, promoting running as a long-term catalyst for a healthy lifestyle.

The ExCom Force

A powerhouse of strategic thinkers and bold leaders driving our mission with clarity and confidence. The core engine behind every big decision.
